**Purpose:**
This service very strongly helps your organization control tasks in internal system team conversation with other teams or all teams. System admin can receive requests form all team thought out one environment, process and reply information or result to user via ms teams chat or email. With templates created in the past, System Admin can reply to users quickly and avoid unexpected situations.

**Note:** In this project I only choose the version trail for 30 days in my organization.
