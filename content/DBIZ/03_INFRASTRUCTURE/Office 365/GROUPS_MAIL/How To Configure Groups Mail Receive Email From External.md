**Purpose:**
With group mail you can add many people in one group, that is suitable for sending email focus. In this case I need group mail receive email message send form out site to my organization, serve for receive logs file send form some system such as monitoring (ElasticSearch/Kiabana/Logstash).  

**I.** Log in account manage office 365 [link](https://admin.microsoft.com/Adminportal/Home?#/homepage):
Choose **Teams & groups** => <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">1</mark> **Active teams & groups** => choose group you want configure in this case i choose <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">2</mark> **SYSTEM_TEAM** => <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">3</mark> **Settings** => tab <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">4 5 6</mark> **Email, Privacy, External file sharing**, choose function expect (choose **Let people outside the organization email** **this team** for receive email form external) => <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">7</mark> **Save**.
